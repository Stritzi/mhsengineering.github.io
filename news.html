Join Colonial Rocketry!

December 3, 2015

Engineering Club is starting a rocketry team, and you can be part of it! We will competing in the Team America Rocketry Challenge. Our goal is to design and build a rocket to carry two eggs to an altitude of 850 feet for about 46 seconds. The Grand Prize winner will receive $100,000! If you are interested in joining, please email the club. Understand that joining is a commitment.
