OUR PROJECTS

Here you can see all of the projects we have done.

Duck Race

October 25, 2016

On September 24th, Morristown Beautiful hosted a clean up event at Burnham Park. The MHS Engineering Club was commissioned by a representative of Morristown Beautiful to create a Duck Race as a part of the barbecue that honored the volunteers of the clean up. Each volunteer was assigned a rubber duck with a number that was then put in the race which started in the lake. The winner was awarded a $50 gift certificate to anywhere in Morristown.
